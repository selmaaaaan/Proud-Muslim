The Mercy of Allah

A man who killed 99 sought repentance, and a scholar assured him of Allah's mercy. Dying en route to a righteous community, Allah forgave him for his sincere intentions.

Story

A man who had killed 99 people sought forgiveness. He approached a scholar and asked if he could repent. The scholar replied, “Who can stand between you and Allah’s mercy?” He advised the man to leave his corrupt surroundings and join a righteous community. On his way, the man passed away, and the angels debated his fate. Allah, in His infinite mercy, judged him by his intentions and granted him forgiveness.

Reflection

Allah’s mercy surpasses all human understanding. How do we turn to Him for forgiveness?

Application

Make a sincere dua for forgiveness and commit to leaving one bad habit behind.

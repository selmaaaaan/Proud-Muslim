Shared Values

Generous

Our God is a generous host, so we have
an abundance mindset.

Humble

God humbles himself by collaborating
with humanity, so we treat every person
as valuable and with something to offer.

Thoughtful

God invites us to live by his wisdom, so
we are both empathetic and strategic.

Creative

God created order out of chaos for life
to flourish, so we innovate.

Unifying

God is creating a worldwide and diverse
human family, so we serve as a catalyst
for its unity.

Unhurried

God rested in his work, so we toil
sustainably and find rest.
